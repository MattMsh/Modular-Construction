A Message From The CEO

Established a decade ago, we made a commitment to sustainable design & construction, while pledging to undertake each Project with our unwavering commitment to Quality and Detail, while preserving the charm and integrity of the design & area.

We are an innovative design and build company specializing in Design Construction of luxury villas & Special Projects across the region. When you decide to retain Modular for your upcoming project, you get industry-recognized professionals with years of experience. Armed with resources that are paramount to a successful completion of your project, we aim to embody our clients vision and bring their dream home to life.

When we take on a project, we explore all possible design options for optimizing your budget and achieving everything on your villa or special project “wish list”.

We continue to take into account the best interests of our clients, especially in terms of cost control, quality control and achieving construction milestones on time and under budget.

We take great pride in having an attentive and detail-oriented project management team that provides consistent communication throughout the entire construction process. We’ll keep you informed through every step of your project.
